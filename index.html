About

About This App

PillLog started as a way to keep track of my own medications, blood pressure, and symptoms after one too many missed doses and forgotten details at doctor visits. What began as a simple pill reminder grew into a full health log — vitals, food, and symptom tracking all in one place, built one piece at a time. It's not fancy, it's not corporate — just a tool built to work the way I needed it to.

About Me

I'm not a professional developer — just someone who got tired of losing track of my own health and decided to build something that actually worked for me. PillLog gets tested on my own life before anyone else's. If it helps you keep better track of yours too, that makes it worth building.

📷 Before You Snap a Photo

This feature sends your photo to Google's free Gemini AI service to read the numbers or identify the food. Google's free tier may review or use photos sent this way to improve their AI models — it's not a fully private connection.

Don't use this if you'd rather not have a photo of you, your medications, or your surroundings looked at outside this app. You can always type things in manually instead — no photo required.
